What is the history of Microlocs?

History reveals the loc hairstyle originated in Egypt, spreading to other countries such as Africa and Asia. Many mummies were discovered with locs. The hairstyle is known for it's spiritual, religious, and cultural reasons, today locs are popular due to social reasons. The technique, style, and size of locs 
have evolved on time. Microlocs are the newly emerging trend of locs.

What is a Microloc?

Small in size, quantity ranging approximately between 150-670, microlocs have proven be to more versatile then traditional locs.


Microlocs difference from Traditional Locs?

The main difference between microlocs and traditional's are the loc size, and quantity. Traditional locs normally doesn't reach a quantity past 150. Also, traditional locs are thicker in size limiting some of the styles attainable by microlocs. Traditional locs range 10mm in diameter, while microlocs range 
6-9mm in diameter. Another differences between Microlocs and Traditional Locs include how they are maintained; interlocking verse retwisting . The two are difference in technique and time. 

How are Microlocs Maintained?

Some people choose to retwist their microlocs, however many interlock there hair. Interlocking requires more time to complete compared to retwisting but results in less maintenance. The interlocking process involves looping ones hair between a "Y" shaped structure of ones hair stains. This process in continued 
until the hair has been gathered and intertwined.
